Humble Beginnings

We are Wilber & Orville Wright. The two us started a small print shop back in 1889. We were soon making our own printing presses that are still reknowned for their craftmanship and quality.

Eventually we began repairing and building bicycles as well. The technical expertise gained here was crucial in our pioneering work in human flight...

Experiments in Aeronautics

We believe the time for practical human flight has come. We built our own wind tunnel to gather experimental data about gliding flight dynamics. This data became instrumental in developing the Wright Glider.

The Wright Flyer

Our Wright Flyer is the state-of-the-art human flight machine. From our humble first flight at Kitty Hawk, which marked mankinds first powered flight, we have since been able to increase our flyers time aloft from seconds to minutes!
